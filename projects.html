My Projects

Non-local Hidden Variables

Bell's Inequality sets a boundary that no local hidden variable theory could replicate Quantum Mechanics, but I approach it as an engineering question to how someone could make a non-local hidden variable theory.

Black hole voids

Hawking radiation decreases the mass of a black hole, which over a long timescale could produce the galactic voids that we observe in the universe.

Non-deterministic Random Algorithm

In this paper, I explore the use of race conditions as a method to produce non-deterministic random variables, and put it to multiple tests of randomness.

The History of Entanglement

In this paper, I go through the evolution of Entanglement from the Einstein Podolsky Rosen paradox, to ramification in quantum computing today.
